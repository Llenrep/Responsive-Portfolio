About Me

Hello! my name is Pernell! My favorite things to do are coding and making music. I first got into as a middle schooler playing the trumpet and eventually it turned into a want to produce music. I guess I suddenly just wanted to attempt putting things together and so far it has been a strong 7 (going on 8 years)!

My interest in coding came from the music which is why I mentioned it first. As I got more into it, it required more and and more power which forced me to learn about computers and little did I realize, it was very interesting. What started off as just normal hardware knowledge slowly developed into software and soon after, I began to expand my knowledge on programs and websites as well as how they were made and what tools are necessary to do so.
